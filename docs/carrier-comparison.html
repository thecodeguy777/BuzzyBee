Why The Other Models Don't Fit

BuzzyHive needs raw materials, not a finished product

What we need

Phone lines and minutes. That's it. We design the dialer, the lead screen, the AI summary, the manager dashboard ourselves. That's where BuzzyHive earns its keep.

Why not RingCentral

Buying RingCentral means putting their phone system in front of our customers. We become a re-seller of their app, not the maker of ours.

Why not Five9

Five9 already has its own agent screen, supervisor screen, and queues. Customers would have to use two systems — Five9 and BuzzyHive — to do one job.

The hidden advantage

SignalWire uses the same playbook as Twilio. If we ever needed to switch, the work is measured in days, not months. The big suites are one-way doors.

Speed To Build

How quickly we can ship features

  • SignalWire — Our first working call happened in a day. Their tools are familiar to anyone who has worked with Twilio.
  • Twilio — Still the gold standard. Most developers know it. We would gladly use it if they let us. They won't.
  • Vonage — Works fine, but the documentation is thinner. More time spent guessing, less time shipping.
  • RingCentral — Not really designed for building on. The instructions assume you're embedding their product, not making your own.
  • Five9 — Their tools are aimed at sales-floor managers configuring queues, not at engineers building new products.

Every week we save here is a week we spend on features customers pay for.

Why SignalWire

Four reasons, any one of which would be enough

1. They said yes

The other four either turned us down or quoted six-to-ten weeks of paperwork. SignalWire approved us, gave us a US phone number, and we are placing calls today.

2. The math works

About 30% cheaper than Twilio for the same call. At our forecast volume, that's a six-figure annual line item we keep instead of paying out.

3. We can leave any time

If SignalWire ever falls short, we can switch our dialer to Twilio in days. We are not locked in.

4. Their roadmap helps us

The next feature SignalWire is shipping lets reps talk through a headset on their computer — no phone bill on the rep's side. Roughly another 40% off Phase 2.
