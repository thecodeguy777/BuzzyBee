02 · Why Now

The market is exploding. The operations are duct-taped.

Most VA agencies are relationship businesses running on a stack of disconnected SaaS tools, with no proprietary infrastructure and below-market pay that drives churn.

  • ✕Fragmented tooling: Google Drive, Time Doctor, Slack and Forms stitched together, never integrated.
  • ✕No proprietary platform: hard to scale, impossible to differentiate on service quality.
  • ✕Below-market VA pay: $400–700/mo drives turnover, which directly hurts client outcomes.
  • ✕Manual, playbook-heavy onboarding: slow ramp, inconsistent delivery.

13 · Go-to-Market

Foundation → Growth → Scale.

1

Foundation · Mo. 1–3

Register entity, ship MVP modules, pre-vet 20–30 candidates, land first 3–5 clients via referral & direct outreach.

2

Growth · Mo. 4–12

Content + paid acquisition to U.S. realtors & solopreneurs, referral program, partnerships, scaling to 15 placements.

3

Scale · Year 2+

Vertical specialization, license the platform (SaaS), add multi-timezone talent markets, build enterprise case studies.

The U.S.-based partner is the single biggest accelerant.

Local presence builds the trust that purely offshore agencies struggle to establish, and unlocks the client relationships that drive recurring revenue.
